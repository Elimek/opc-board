⚙️ HOW IT WORKS

Three Modes, One System

三种模式，一个系统

1

Weekly Board Meeting

每周董事会

30 minutes. Present your challenge. Each advisor responds with their framework. You synthesize.

30分钟。提出当前挑战，每个部门用各自框架给出意见，你做决策。

2

Daily OS Routine

每日操作系统

Morning protocol (Huberman). Deep work blocks (Dan Koe). Evening review. Your body is the engine.

早晨协议(Huberman) + 深工作(Dan Koe) + 晚间复盘。身体是公司的硬件。

3

5-Filter Decision Protocol

五滤镜决策

Run every decision through: Strategy → Finance → Health → Marketing → Vision. Never decide alone.

每个决策过五关：战略→财务→健康→营销→愿景。一个人不做重大决定。
