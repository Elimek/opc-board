# 🧬 Health CEO — Personal Biology Operating System

## Core Mental Models

### 1. The Body Is Infrastructure (Bryan Johnson)
Your body is the hardware your company runs on. Every bad meal, missed sleep, or skipped workout is technical debt. Bryan treats his body as an engineering project — measure everything, optimize continuously. The goal: biological age < chronological age.

### 2. Circadian Foundation (Huberman)
All health starts with light. Morning sunlight signals your entire biology. Get 5-10 minutes of morning sun (before 10am) to set your circadian clock. This affects sleep, energy, focus, and mood for the entire day.

### 3. The Protocol Stack (Both)
Don't optimize one thing in isolation. Bryan's "Power Laws": First principles of health in order:
1. Don't smoke
2. Don't drink excessively
3. Sleep 8+ hours
4. Eat whole foods
5. Exercise daily
Master the basics before touching supplements.

### 4. Stress = Growth + Recovery (Huberman)
Stress isn't the enemy — incomplete recovery is. Exercise stresses the body → sleep recovers → adaptation. Mental stress → NSDR (Non-Sleep Deep Rest) → resilience. The formula: stress + recovery = growth.

### 5. Data Over Feelings (Bryan)
"Don't guess. Measure." Bryan tracks: sleep (Oura/Ring), blood markers (quarterly), body composition (DEXA), VO2 max, biological age. You don't need all of that. Start with: sleep quality, resting heart rate, HRV, and one blood panel per year.

## Anti-Patterns / What NOT To Do

- ❌ Skip sleep to "grind" — this destroys everything else
- ❌ Chase supplements before fixing diet and sleep
- ❌ Overtrain without recovery — stress without rest = burnout
- ❌ Drink coffee immediately after waking (blocks adenosine clearing)
- ❌ Use bright screens after 10pm

## Daily Protocol (Simplified)

### Morning (6:00-8:00)
- ☀️ 5-10 min sunlight (or bright light device)
- 🚫 No caffeine for first 90 min
- 💧 Water with electrolytes
- 🧠 60-90 min deep work block

### Midday (12:00-14:00)
- 🥗 Whole food lunch (protein + veggies)
- 🚶 10-20 min walk (or NSDR)

### Afternoon (14:00-18:00)
- 🏋️ Exercise (strength 3x/week, cardio 2x/week, mobility daily)
- 🥤 No caffeine after 3pm

### Evening (18:00-22:00)
- 🍽️ Dinner early (last meal 3h before sleep)
- 📵 Dim lights, no screens 60 min before bed
- ❄️ Cold shower or face cold plunge

### Night (22:00-6:00)
- 😴 8+ hours sleep, same time every night (non-negotiable)
- 📊 Data: sleep score, HRV, resting heart rate
