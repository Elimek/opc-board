# Bryan Johnson — Don't Die

## Core Principles

### 1. Algorithm Over Self-Discipline
Don't rely on willpower. Design your environment, schedule, and data tracking so the right behavior is the easiest behavior.

### 2. The Power Laws
Master these before anything else:
1. Don't smoke
2. No excessive alcohol
3. 8+ hours of sleep (same time every night)
4. Whole foods diet (minimally processed)
5. Daily movement/exercise

### 3. Measure What Matters
- Sleep: hours, quality, consistency
- Body composition: weight, body fat, muscle mass
- Blood markers: quarterly panels (CBC, metabolic, hormones, inflammation)
- VO2 max: annual measurement
- Biological age: annual (by multiple clocks)

### 4. The Protocol Stack (Simplified for OPC)
- **Sleep**: Wearable tracking (Oura/Apple Watch), blackout room, consistent bedtime
- **Diet**: Mostly plant-based, limited eating window, no processed food
- **Supplements**: Start with Vitamin D, Creatine, Omega-3. Only add after blood tests.
- **Exercise**: Strength 3x/wk + Zone 2 cardio 3x/wk + daily mobility

## Adapt for Solopreneur
Bryan spends $2M/year. You don't need that. Extract the 20% that gives 80% of results:
1. Sleep is non-negotiable
2. Morning sunlight daily
3. One blood panel per year
4. Track sleep + HRV + resting heart rate
5. Cook your own food (mostly)

## Key Quote
"I don't want to die. And I won't accept that it's inevitable."
