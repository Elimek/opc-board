# Andrew Huberman — Daily Protocols

## Core Principles

### 1. Circadian Biology is Everything
Light sets your entire physiological clock. Morning sunlight (within 30-60 min of waking) sets cortisol, melatonin, alertness, and mood for the day.

### 2. The 90-Minute Focus Block
The brain can sustain deep focus for ~90 minutes. After that, cognitive performance drops. Structure your work around this natural rhythm.

### 3. Stress + Recovery = Growth
- Exercise stress → sleep recovery → adaptation
- Mental challenge → NSDR → learning consolidation
- Cold exposure → dopamine increase → sustained focus

### 4. Delayed Caffeine Protocol
Caffeine blocks adenosine (the sleepiness chemical). If you drink it immediately upon waking, you're blocking the clearing of sleep pressure. Wait 90-120 minutes after waking for your first caffeine.

## Key Protocols

### Morning
- ☀️ 5-10 min sunlight (no sunglasses)
- 🚫 No caffeine for 90 min
- ❄️ 1-3 min cold shower (optional, for dopamine)
- 🧠 Deep work block (90 min)

### Afternoon
- NSDR (10-20 min) or nap after lunch
- Walk (10 min) for post-meal glucose control

### Evening
- View sunset (sets circadian clock)
- Dim lights 1-2 hours before bed
- No bright screens after 10pm

## Key Quote
"The best protocol is the one you'll actually do."
